About Us
Who We Are
We are a group of Ann Arbor-area residents, affiliated with Ann Arbor Christian Reformed Church, who have a deep concern for the well-being of the international students and scholars in our community. In this current climate of geo-political tensions, travel bans, and the Covid-19 pandemic, we know that many of you may be feeling uncertain, anxious or even fearful about being here. We would like to help in any way possible to create a safe space where you and all our international friends will feel welcomed.
What We Do
We offer a variety of ways to connect with Americans, both learning from them and sharing with them. Our offering ranges from individual, one-on-one meetings to larger discussion groups, activities for Children and Familes, and more. Click below to learn more about what we do.
