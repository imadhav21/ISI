About Us/Our Mission
Who We Are
We are a group of Ann Arbor-area residents who have a deep concern for the well-being of the international students and scholars in our community. In this current climate of geo-political tensions, travel bans, and the Covid-19 pandemic, we know that many of them may be feeling uncertain, anxious or even fearful about being here. We would like to help in any way possible to create a safe space where our international friends feel welcomed.
